## What we collect

When you submit the email form or survey, we collect:

- The information you provide (e.g. email, company name, role, answers).
- Basic technical metadata (timestamp, page submitted from).

We do not use third-party analytics or trackers on this site.

## Why we collect it

- To respond to you.
- To understand the AEWV compliance landscape so we can build the right product.

## How long we keep it

Survey responses: until we have finished the validation phase, then aggregated and deleted in raw form within twelve months of collection.

## Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you can ask us what we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Email hello@aewvcompliance.co.nz.

## Sharing

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with third parties except where required by law.
